About Me


I have more of a traditional art background, as I was in Talented Art through Middle and High School. After taking a few photoshop courses thorugh high school, I decided to take my skills to the University of New Orleans where I graduated with a degree in Fine Arts where my concentration was Digital Art where we used programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects to create art.

Through college I worked at Office Depot where I flip flopped between working in the technology sales area and working in the Copy, Print, & Design area. In the Copy, Print, & Design area, I was essentially the the sole person responsible for helping customers design their business cards, clean up the art for their business, format their prints, as well as give creative advice to achieve the final product they were looking for.
