About Me


My girlfriend Aubrey and I at a recent Painting with a Twist outing.

I have more of a traditional art background, as I was in the Talented Art Program through middle and high school. After taking a few photoshop courses high school, I decided to take my skills to the University of New Orleans. I graduated with a degree in Fine Arts with a concentration in Digital Art where we used programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects to create art.

Through college, I worked at Office Depot where I floated between the technology sales area and Copy, Print, & Design department. In the Copy, Print, & Design area, I was essentially the sole person responsible for helping customers design their business cards, clean up the art for their business, format their prints, as well as give creative advice to achieve the final product they were looking for.
